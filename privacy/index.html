Privacy Policy

Green Pine RC Internal Tools
Effective date: May 1, 2025

1. Internal Use Only

This application is an internal business tool operated exclusively by Green Pine RC. It is not offered to the public and does not collect information from end consumers.

2. Data Accessed

This tool connects to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API to access company financial data, including invoices, transactions, projects, and profit & loss information. This data is used solely for internal bookkeeping and financial reporting purposes.

3. Data Storage

No QuickBooks data is stored permanently by this application. Data is retrieved at the time of use and is not logged, cached to external systems, or retained beyond the active session.

4. Data Sharing

No data accessed through this tool is shared with third parties. All information remains within Green Pine RC's internal operations.

5. OAuth Tokens

OAuth access tokens issued by Intuit are stored securely and used only to authenticate requests on behalf of the authorized Green Pine RC account. Tokens are not shared or transmitted to any party other than Intuit's API endpoints.

6. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact Green Pine RC at maynard.colin@pm.me.
